Privacy Policy

Offline Internet ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. No Account Required

The App does not require registration or account creation. You can use all features without signing up or providing personal details.

2. Personal Data Collection

The developer of the App does not collect or store personal information.

The developer does not collect or store:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Photos or files
  • Precise location data

Your searches and activities remain on your device.

3. Search Queries

Search queries entered inside Offline Internet are processed locally on your device when using offline mode. The developer does not receive, store, or transmit your queries.

4. Internet Access & Web Browsing

When internet mode is used, the App opens web pages through an in-app browser (WebView). External websites are responsible for their own content and privacy practices.

The App itself does not monitor, record, or store the websites you visit.

When online search is used, requests are sent directly from your device to external search providers and websites. These requests are not routed through or stored by the developer.

5. Offline Knowledge Data

The App allows optional downloading of publicly available open knowledge datasets (for example, encyclopedia content). These files are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to the developer.

6. Data Storage

All generated content, cached information, and downloaded datasets remain stored locally on your device. The developer has no access to this data.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Security

Because the App does not store personal information on external servers, your data primarily remains on your device.

9. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services necessary for functionality:

  • External websites accessed through the in-app browser

These services have their own privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect feature or policy changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

12. Data Security and Transmission

All communications performed by third-party services (such as external websites) use industry-standard encrypted connections (HTTPS). The application itself does not transmit personal data to the developer's servers.
